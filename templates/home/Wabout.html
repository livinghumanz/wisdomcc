Wisdom Coaching Center was proudly established on June 20, 2018. For the comfort of the students, the management has furnished the institute distinctively from other surrounding institutions with School like infrastructure, Hassle free power supply, first aid kit, drinking water, telephone facilities to communicate with parents, Photocopy machine, Rest Room facility and so on. For the benefits of the students, Wisdom ensures by taking various efforts for the students’ academic growth in such way of conducting all seven days class in a week, unit wise portion lecturing, subject-wise examination, drawing the revised examination paper for parental attention, parent’s & teachers’ meeting, tracking & letting their ward’s academic performance and many more.
